Privacy Policy for Gamefinity Giveaway Contest

Effective Date: 25/12/24.

Introduction

Welcome to Gamefinity Giveaway Contest. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you participate in our giveaways, contests, and use our Website. By accessing or using our Website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information: personal information and non-personal information.

Personal Information: When you enter our giveaway contest, we may collect personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Age
  • Social media profiles

    • This information is necessary for contacting winners, verifying eligibility, and administering the contest.

    Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information that does not identify you personally. This includes:

  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Pages viewed
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (to improve user experience)

    • 2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To enter you into our giveaway contest and communicate with you about the contest.
  • To verify your eligibility for the contest and prize.
  • To contact you in the event you are a winner.
  • To send you promotional material, newsletters, or other marketing communications, if you have opted in.
  • To analyze Website usage and improve the user experience.

    • 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    Our Website may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These tools help us:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Understand how visitors interact with our Website.
  • Analyze traffic and trends to improve our Website.

    • You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some features of the Website.

    4. How We Protect Your Information

    We take reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include secure servers, encryption, and restricted access to your information. However, no security system is completely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

    5. Sharing Your Information

    We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your consent. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate our Website, conduct the contest, and deliver prizes (e.g., email providers, shipping companies).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process, we may disclose your information to comply with regulations or in response to lawful requests by public authorities.

    • 6. Third-Party Links

    Our Website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

    7. Your Choices

    You have several rights and choices regarding your personal information:

  • Opt-out of Communications: If you no longer wish to receive marketing communications, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.
  • Access and Correction: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate.
  • Data Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

    • To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at gamefinity@gmail.com.

    8. Children's Privacy

    Our Website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

    9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

    If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
